Frequently Asked Questions



1. What types of coffee do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of coffee options, including espresso, cappuccino, latte, drip coffee, and more. Our menu has something for everyone's taste.

2. Are your coffee beans sourced ethically?

Yes, we take pride in sourcing our coffee beans from sustainable and ethically responsible suppliers. We strive to support fair trade and environmentally conscious practices.

3. Can I customize my coffee order?

Absolutely! We understand that everyone has unique preferences. You can customize your coffee order by selecting the type of coffee, milk, sweeteners, and additional flavors according to your taste.

4. Do you offer dairy-free or vegan options?

Yes, we provide dairy-free milk alternatives such as almond milk, soy milk, and oat milk. Our goal is to cater to various dietary preferences and restrictions.

5. How can I place an order for pickup?

To place an order for pickup, simply visit our website or mobile app, browse the menu, select your desired items, and proceed to the checkout. Choose the pickup option and select your preferred pickup time.
